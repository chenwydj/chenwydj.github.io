Research Interest


  • [A] Aims: Closing the Gap between DL Theory and DL Applications
    I seek to understand deep neural networks and close the gap between the theory and application side. Specifically: (1) how theoretical findings help improve architecture/optimization/generalization; (2) how empirical observations in deep learning inspire the direction of theory research.
  • [B] Bridges: Neural Architecture Search, Learning-Augmented Optimization, Neural Tangent Kernel
    I am passionate about the rising field of: (1) Neural Architecture Search (NAS) -- study the impact of diverse architectures in a high-dimensional search space; (2) Learning-to-optimize (L2O) -- whether we can automate the adaptive gradient descent; (3) Neural Tangent Kernel (NTK) -- characterize generalization and training dynamics of (wide) deep networks. They are both profound research topics and can also bridge the gap between the theory and application side!
  • [C] Challenges: Computer Vision, AutoML, Sparse Networks
    I have broad interests in real-world applications: pretrained vision models, Automated Machine Learning (AutoML), finding/training sparse networks, contrastive learning, etc. These areas are still near blank pages of DL theory, but are also cutting joints between the theories and applications!
